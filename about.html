About Me

Julie Sakai

Julie is originally from Hawai’i and moved to Florida in 2019. She is currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a focus on Software Development at Bellevue University through the Disney Aspire program. While pursuing her degree, Julie works as a Disney Professional Intern in Assistive Technology, where she supports services for Guests with disabilities. She is on track to graduate as a senior at Bellevue University July 2025.

Julie began her Disney journey in 2019 as a Disney College Program participant, working as a Merchandise Cast Member at Disney's Polynesian Resort. She soon transitioned to a role in Entertainment as a Character Performer, showcasing her talent and enthusiasm. In 2021, Julie returned to the Walt Disney Company as an Attractions Cast Member at Epcot, where she worked on Journey into Imagination with Figment and the Pixar Short Film Festival. She soon had the opportunity to be part of the Attraction opening team for Remy’s Ratatouille Adventure at Epcot.

After gaining valuable experience in Attractions, Julie returned to Entertainment as a Character Performer. She later accepted a position as an Epcot Guest Relations Hostess. She currently working as an Assistive Technology Intern, supporting Services for Guests with Disabilities. Julie is passionate about strengthening her technical and creative skills to make a lasting impact at Disney. She is excited to pursue a long-term career in IT within the Walt Disney company.
